Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the consultation really free?

Yes — your initial consultation is completely complimentary with no financial commitment required. This includes a full exam, diagnostic records, digital smile simulation, and personalized treatment plan.

At what age should my child have their first orthodontic evaluation?

The American Association of Orthodontics recommends a first evaluation by age 7, typically after the first set of permanent molars has erupted (around ages 6–8). However, Dr. Smith sees children as young as 4 when early concerns are present — such as mouth breathing, speech delays, or jaw development issues.

Am I too old for orthodontic treatment?

Not at all — about 30% of our patients are adults. Orthodontic treatment is effective at any age, and modern options like Invisalign make it more discreet and lifestyle-friendly than ever. Many adults find that straightening their teeth improves not just their smile, but their confidence and overall dental health.

How long does treatment take?

Treatment duration varies based on complexity. Minor alignment issues can be corrected in as few as 6 months, while comprehensive treatment typically ranges from 12–24 months. Dr. Smith will give you a personalized timeline estimate during your free consultation.

Do you accept my insurance?

We work with most PPO dental insurance plans and provide full assistance with billing and claims submission. Even if you're uninsured, our flexible in-house payment plans and financing partners (CareCredit and Cherry) make treatment affordable. Call us at (301) 307-5115 and we'll verify your benefits before your visit.

Can I start treatment the same day as my consultation?

In many cases, yes. If you're ready to move forward and the clinical situation allows, Dr. Smith can begin treatment on the same day as your consultation — so you leave with your new smile journey already underway.
