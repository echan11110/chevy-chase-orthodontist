Meet Our Founder

Dr. Jennifer Smith, DMD

Dr. Smith's passion for orthodontics began as an orthodontic patient herself — an experience that gave her a firsthand understanding of how transformative a great smile can be. That commitment deepened through volunteer work at the Children's Hospital of Philadelphia's Craniofacial Anomalies Clinic.

Board-certified by the American Board of Orthodontics and an Invisalign Platinum Plus Provider, Dr. Smith continuously pursues the forefront of her field — from dental sleep medicine and airway health to facial development, orofacial myofunctional therapy, and TMJ treatment.

Areas of Expertise

Dr. Smith's training spans well beyond traditional orthodontics.

Orthodontics

Traditional braces and clear aligner treatment for patients of all ages.

Dental Sleep Medicine

Airway Health

Promoting nasal breathing and healthy airway development for lifelong wellness.

Facial Development

Guiding optimal craniofacial growth patterns in children and teens.

Orofacial Myofunctional Therapy

Correcting muscle pattern disorders that affect oral function and facial growth.

TMJ Treatment

Evidence-based care for jaw pain, headaches, and temporomandibular dysfunction.
